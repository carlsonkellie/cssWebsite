Affiliates

The Computer Science Society is one of many student organizations aiming to promote computer science at the University of Pennsylvania. Check out some of the other organizations below!


PennApps

Hosted at the nation's first university, PennApps is the original college hackathon. Come join us to learn something new, build an app, or start a company. Let's continue to make history together!

For more information, please visit pennapps.com

PClassic

The Philadelphia Classic (PClassic) is a semesterly programming competition for high school students hosted by the Computer Science Society at the University of Pennsylvania. Prizes and awards are given out to the top scoring teams, and registration for the competition is free for all high schools.

For more information, please visit pclassic.org

Architects

The Architechs are responsible for hardware hacking at Penn. We organize the hardware portion of PennApps, providing hackers with parts, mentorship, and all the hardware resources they need. We also organize bootcamps and seminars throughout the year to help students get more familiar with various hardware platforms.

For more information, please visit architechs.seas.upenn.edu
