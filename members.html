Membership

All undergraduate students in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Computer and Cognitive Science, Computational Biology, Digital Media Design, Networked& Social Systems Engineering, or interested in the field of computer science are automatically a member of CSS. This includes but is not limited to all majors, minors, dual degrees, and anyone simply interested in learning more about computer science. All CSS members are always welcome to attend our public events. Learn more about how to get involved in CSS!

The Computer Science Society operates as an executive board and three committees, with two board members leading each committee.
